Accumulated points do not constitute property of the Subscriber. Points accumulated by a Subscriber are for the Subscriber's benefit only.

Duplicate accounts: If more than one account is assigned to an individual Subscriber, he/she will receive points for one account only.

For example, if a Subscriber purchases an eligible Image for $100 then the Subscriber’s account will be awarded 5 reward points.

Points must be redeemed or used prior to 30 months from the date of the transaction in which such points were awarded to the Subscriber’s account. Points not used prior to 30 months will expire on a per transaction basis after 30 months of the transaction date.

Subscriber is responsible for ensuring that your points are properly credited. If proper credit does not appear on your account at www.cpimages, the Subscriber should contact archive@cpimages.com.

The AEIS Transaction Fee will be added on each transaction. (The AEIS Transaction Fee can be changed at any time.)

No refund will be made with respect to points remaining on cards after such date. AEIS may change the terms of the Participant Agreement at its sole discretion and without notice to you.
